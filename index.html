about

Jacob Samuel is a front-end web developer, designer, and photographer, with experience working with HTML5, Javascript, CSS3, and C#. His background in visual design, and freelance web development inform his creative but strategic approach. Jacob is fueled by his passion for understanding the nuances of how the world works. He considers himself a "forever student", eager to both build on his skills in web development and design, and his emotional inteligence in order to understand the world around him more effectively to bring creative ideas and visions to life. His hunger for knowledge and determination to solve problems has contributed to his success as a developer, father, partner, photographer, and team member. Jacob believes mindfulness in the workplace is key to success - a tenet he lives out through his interests in yoga, meditation, gardening, and painting. Jacob is currently working as a freelance developer and photographer and is always up for a challenge. Reach out to jacobdustinsamuel@gmail.com to connect!
